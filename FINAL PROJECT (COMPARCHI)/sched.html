Preemptive Scheduling

Algorithms where the CPU can be taken away from a process before it completes. Includes Round Robin and SRTF with preemption.

Non-Preemptive Scheduling

Algorithms where processes run to completion once started. Includes FCFS and SJF Scheduling without preemption.
